Benefits of Green Space

The need for healthy and properly maintained green space is not only ornamental or aesthetic, it has many benefits for human health and the environment.

Water Purification

Properly maintained lawns filter rainwater and reduce pollution runoff.

Air Purification

Grass captures dust, produces oxygen and improves air quality.

Erosion Control

Strong root systems prevent soil erosion and water runoff.

Temperature Control

Green spaces reduce heat island effect in urban areas.

Physical Activity

Green spaces encourage outdoor recreation and exercise.

Stress Reduction

Natural environments help reduce stress and improve mental wellbeing.

Healthy, properly maintained green space provides substantial benefits to the environment in terms of erosion control, water purification, air purification, temperature modification, energy and cost saving, oxygen generation and carbon sequestration.

Green space provides substantial benefits to human health in terms of recreation, increased physical activity, reduced risk of obesity and healthcare, and stress reduction.
