Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What areas in Montreal do you service?

We serve all areas of Montreal Island, including Westmount, NDG, Outremont, Mile End, Plateau, Saint-Laurent, Ahuntsic, Anjou, and Dorval. We also service some areas in Laval and the South Shore.

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our office directly at 514-369-3693.

How do I schedule a service or get an estimate?

The easiest way to schedule service or request an estimate is through our free estimate form. Alternatively, you can call our office or send us an email with your information and service needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, e-transfers, checks, and cash. For recurring services, we offer convenient automatic payment options.

Do you offer emergency lawn care services?

Yes, we understand that lawn issues can sometimes require immediate attention. For urgent matters like pest outbreaks or storm damage, please call our office directly, and we'll do our best to accommodate your needs as quickly as possible.
