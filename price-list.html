Our Pricing Structure

At Plaisir Vert, we believe in complete transparency when it comes to pricing. The rates listed below are starting prices based on average property sizes in Montreal. For a precise quote tailored to your specific property, please request a free estimate.

We offer competitive pricing while maintaining the highest quality standards and using only ecological products. Various discount options are available for seasonal packages, regular maintenance contracts, and referrals.

Important Notes

  • All prices are in Canadian dollars (CAD) and exclude applicable taxes.
  • Prices may vary based on property size, condition, accessibility, and specific requirements.
  • Discounts available for multiple services, seasonal contracts, and referrals.
  • Additional charges may apply for difficult access, excessive debris, or special conditions.
